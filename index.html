About Us

We are more than just a manufacturer of mechs; we are a beacon of innovation and excellence in the universe. Our journey began with a focus on the finest in ordnance and defensive systems, but it was our commitment to our pilots that truly set us apart. Recognizing the need for pilots to have the most advanced technology at their disposal, we embarked on a transformative path, expanding our product line to include frontline frames that redefine power and dominance.

At the heart of our mission is a deep-seated belief in the potential of our pilots and the incredible potential of our mechs. Our models, such as the Barbabrossa, Genghis, Iskander, Napoleon, Saladin, Sherman, Tokugawa, Gilgamesh, and Sunzi, are not just machines; they are symbols of strength and dominance. Each frame is designed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring robustness and overwhelming performance. We understand that piloting a mech is more than just operating a machine; it's a calling, a way of life. That's why we invest in our pilots, providing them with the best training, support, and equipment to ensure they can perform at their peak.

Harrison Armory is not just a company; it's a family. We are a family that's dedicated to creating the future of warfare, one mech at a time. We are here to support our pilots, to help them achieve their full potential, and to ensure that they have the tools and the technology they need to make a difference.

Join us on this journey, where the future of warfare meets the present. At Harrison Armory, we are committed to excellence, to innovation, and to our pilots. Together, we will redefine the battlefield.
